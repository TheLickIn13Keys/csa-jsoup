Amazon Customer
3.0 out of 5 starsGreat shoes, but stitching on toes wearing through after less than a year of use.
Reviewed in the United States on February 10, 2024
These shoes are very comfortable and are also attractive enough to wear almost anywhere. Soft inner soles are comfortable and feel great after walking and standing. However, the stitching on the front of the toes on both shoes is coming loose after the shoes were only 10 months old. Appears that threads inside of the shoe are broken and letting the outside thread to come out in continuous thread on outside of the shoes. Need to use a better thread material, ruining otherwise very wearable shoes. Disappointing that will have to replace the shoes after such a short time of use. Love the shoes otherwise, but afraid to spend $$ on another pair that will also last less than 1 year just around the house and on short trips in the car.
